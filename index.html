📊 What is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market is a financial platform where users buy or sell contracts based on the outcomes of real-world events. Each share's price represents the market's collective belief in that event's probability.

Price = Probability: If a share trades at $0.75, the market estimates a 75% chance the event will occur. If the event happens, the share pays out $1; otherwise, $0.

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Step 1: Sign up and verify your identity.
  2. Step 2: Deposit funds via UPI, crypto, or card.
  3. Step 3: Pick an event (e.g., “Will India ban TikTok in 2025?”)
  4. Step 4: Buy “Yes” or “No” shares depending on your belief.
  5. Step 5: Wait for the outcome. If you're right, you earn ₹1/share.
