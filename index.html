What is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market is a financial exchange where users buy and sell shares of future events. Each share’s price reflects the market’s collective belief in that outcome’s probability.

For example, if the event “Will SpaceX land Starship on Mars by 2030?” trades at $0.22, the market estimates a 22% chance of success.

How It Works

  1. Define an Event: e.g., “Will India’s GDP grow above 6% in 2025?”
  2. Buy or Sell Shares: “Yes” shares pay ₹1 if true, ₹0 if not.
  3. Interpret Prices: ₹0.70 means a 70% chance of occurring.
  4. Track & Trade: Market prices shift as new information emerges.
  5. Settle: When the event resolves, correct predictions pay out ₹1 per share.
